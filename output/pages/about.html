About

I’m a JavaScript full-stack web developer looking to transition into the field of machine learning and AI. Currently, I’m self-studying full-time and enrolled in the MITx MicroMasters in Statistics and Data Science program and Udacity’s Deep Reinforcement Learning Nanodegree program. I have graduated from Udacity’s Machine Learning Engineer, Natural Language Processing, and Artificial Intelligence Nanodegree programs in 2018.

Prior to this goal of self-study, I developed various JavaScript web applications at Sudokrew for clients in the education and energy industries, and beforehand, worked at Boeing for two years, developing and maintaining C# and Java web applications for internal customers. I graduated from DevLeague, Hawaii’s first coding bootcamp, in 2015 and from the University of Hawaiʻi at Mānoa with a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science in 2012.
